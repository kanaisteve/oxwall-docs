# Theme Project

First of all, you need to understand Oxwall theme structure. Below is what the structure of Oxwall themes look like:

**ThemeName**
    |
    |_ theme.xml
    |_ base.css
    |_ README.md
    |_ **images**
    |_ **master_pages**
       |_ blank.html
       |_ dndindex.html.html
       |_ general.html.html
       |_ html_document.html
       |_ admin.html
    |_ **decorators**
    |_ **mobile**
        |_ **fonts**
        |_ **images**
        |_ **master_pages**
        |_ base.css
    |_ theme.jpg
    |_ theme_preview.jpg


**Bolded words are folders**
